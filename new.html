A Soft Murmur

Check out a cool website A Soft Murmur!

About a year ago the internet was taken by storm by these oddly satisfying videos called ASMR. ASMR is a feeling of well-being combined with a tingling sensation in the scalp and down the back of the neck, as experienced by some people in response to a specific gentle stimulus, often a particular sound. A soft murmur is a website to help wash away distractions with some ASMR sounds. Some sounds include rain, thunder. Waves, wind, fire, birds, crickets, coffee shop, singing bowl, and white noise just to name a few! Right when I opened up this website I thought it was the coolest thing! With a sleek black background and the cutest little icons with the sounds right there a click away! Each icon has its own sound and also its own volume knob.

The maker of this website says “I created A Soft Murmur for myself. I like to work in public spaces like libraries and coffee shops, but they can be noisy and distracting. Listening to ambient helps me focus, and I like being able to mix different sounds together to match the environment I’m in.” From this little website he has found a lot of success! On this website there is also a little blog, it’s not much but it says to keep checking in for more information. This was written in 2017, and this was my least favorite part of this website. I think that Gabriel Brady (The designer of this website) could have had a lot more success if he updates sounds and even wrote funny little blogs about new sound or even ASMR sounds.

From looking at is from this class view point it seems like something I could do with just a little bit more knowledge on web design. The one thing that I would really struggle with when making this is getting those icons, the volume buttons and obviously the sound!

Something really cool about this site is EVERYTHING on it is written, drawn and even coded by Matthem Inman- and he is “The Oatmeal”. He currently lives in Seattle, Washington with his two dogs Rambo and Beatrix. When I went to learn more about this site I saw that the creator doesn’t give out his email but allows fans to post on his Facebook page, and that seemed pretty unique!

When looking at this site you totally could spend hours on it, and thinking that the creator does it all is even more impressive. It is filled with little comics that are guaranteed to make anyone smile! This site unlike the other one had blogs that were up to date!
