About Helena's Cup Cake Shop:

The Helena Cupcake Shop

Here at The Helena Cupcake Shop we’ve been baking from scratch each and every morning for over 10 years! Our bakers start with the highest quality, freshest ingredients available and rise early to prepare breakfast pastries, sweet and savory confections, cookies, bars, brownies, and of course specialty cupcakes daily. We also offer seasonal ice cream sandwiches, homemade marshmallows and some vegan/gluten free options in house as well as a full line of locally roasted coffee and espresso drinks for you to enjoy in the cafe, on our patio or for carry out.

Our daily menu features a wide variety of cupcakes and scratch baked confections in many traditional and modern flavors. We offer a rotation of seasonal goods, gift items, and feature a new flavor each week! Sweet delicacies and special occasion cakes for any event are available by custom order so give us a call and let us help with your celebration!
